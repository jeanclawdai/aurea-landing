# Trenches Battleground Style Guide

## Overview
This is the refined art style for "Trenches Battleground" - a military strategy game concept art.

## Rendering Style
- **Medium:** Hand-painted digital illustration
- **Style:** Comic book / graphic novel / concept art hybrid
- **Rendering:** Semi-realistic with visible brushwork, NOT photorealistic, NOT anime
- **Linework:** Bold dark ink outlines with varying weight (thicker on silhouettes, thinner on details)
- **Cross-hatching:** Used for skin texture, fabric, shadows - NOT smooth gradients
- **Texture:** Visible brush strokes, NOT over-smoothed

## Color Palette
| Role | Colors |
|------|--------|
| **Background Base** | Muted warm: parchment yellows, dusty pinks, tan, warm browns |
| **Atmosphere** | Purple/violet haze (dominant) |
| **Focal Point (Ally)** | Saturated cyan-blue (#00AAFF, #3DE0FF) |
| **Focal Point (Enemy)** | Hot red/crimson (#CC2222) |
| **Tech/Glow** | Neon green LED (#00FF66) |
| **Shadows** | Near-black with purple undertone |

## Lighting
- **Primary:** Light emanates FROM the glowing objects (chess pieces) - blue/cyan cast on nearby objects
- **Secondary:** Warm light from upper right
- **Rim lighting:** Blue edge light on fingers/objects from glow
- **Atmospheric:** Purple haze throughout - dust motes visible in light beams
- **Shadows:** Soft, warm under hand; cool blue near glowing objects

## Key Visual Elements

### The Chess Pieces
- **Blue King** = Ally/Player - glowing cyan with question mark
- **Red King** = Enemy
- Smooth, reflective surface (like polished metal/glass)
- Bright white highlights, deep color in shadows
- Glowing energy rings around placement points

### The Map/Table
- Aged parchment texture with cracks, creases
- Grid lines (tactical map style)
- Hand-drawn terrain: trenches, rocky formations
- "Trenches Battleground" text - handwritten/rough brush style
- Converging arrows pointing to center

### The Hand (Close-up)
- Older, weathered skin (commander)
- Cross-hatching for wrinkles, tendons
- Exaggerated but anatomically correct
- Blue rim light from chess piece glow

### Environment
- Dark, vague architectural shapes in background
- Bunker/war room atmosphere
- Always atmospheric haze (purple)

## Composition
- Cinematic 16:9 widescreen
- Rule of thirds / golden ratio placement
- Diagonal lines (hand descending)
- Foreground-middleground-background layering
- Vignette darkening at edges


## Additional Style Notes (from new references)
- **Watercolor backgrounds** - loose, painterly quality with wet-on-wet blending
- **Text elements** - bold sans-serif lettering, "THE UNKNOWN MOVE" style captions
- **Scale contrast** - enormous hand vs tiny soldiers for godlike strategist feel
- **Medieval/steampunk gauntlet** - armored tactical glove with metallic details
- **Sepia ink tones** - map rendered like aged military document
- **Barbed wire and trenches** - X-shaped obstacles on battlefield

## What NOT To Do
- ❌ Bright saturated environments
- ❌ Photorealistic rendering
- ❌ Smooth gradients / over-smoothed
- ❌ Anime/cartoon proportions
- ❌ Clean vector lines
- ❌ Daylight/sunny scenes
- ❌ Flat colors without texture
